THE POLITICAL FRAMEWORK

About the Luminent System

A public system where authority must be supported by knowledge, ethical conduct and demonstrated contribution.

Thesis-Based Governance

Every major public proposal begins as a thesis. It is examined through anti-thesis, evidence, citizen response and institutional debate. A final synthesis is developed for public decision-making.

The Luminent acts as the principal constitutional guide, while the Council of Manu represents major fields of knowledge and public administration.
